Aloo Curry

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 4 members
Ingredients Quantity
potatoes 250 gms
onions 2
chilli 4 pieces
ginger 5 gm
oil 5 gms
salt as required

Step 1:cut potatoes and onions into small pieces.
Step 2:make ginger and chilli into paste.
Step 3:switch on the stove and add some oil to pan along with some temper.
Step 4:now pour the vegetable pieces into it.then add required amount of salt and wait for 8 min.
Step 5: now add the paste you made along with some turmeric powder.mix it for 2 min.and your delicious aloo curry is ready to taste.

Gherkin Fry

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 4 members
Ingredients Quantity
gherkins 250 gms
onions 2
red chilli powder table spoon
oil 5 gms
salt as required

Step 1:cut gherkins and onions into small pieces seperately.
Step 2: switch on the stove and add 250 gm of oiland wait for 2 min.
Step 3:after oil gets heated,add gerkin pieces into it and after some time add onion pieces into it.
Step 4: after some time drain out all the oil in pan and add salt and chilli powder to it and mix it.
Step 5:thats it! your delicious dish is ready.
